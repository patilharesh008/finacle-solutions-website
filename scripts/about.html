About Us

SAMARTH ENTERPRISES, was conceived in 1992 with a vision to cater to the growing demand for Flexible Packaging Film such as Antistatic Film, Shrink Film and Stretch Film, Mulching Film, Poly Tubing, Poly Bag, Air Bubble film/Roll, Air Bubble Bag, BOPP Tapes, Foam Rolls & Bags etc. In few years it had emerged as a leader in Multilayer & monolayer flexible packaging film. On the anvil are ambitious plans for capacity addition and new extrusion plant addition over and above the existing.

Team SAMARTH ENTERPRISES is lead by Mr. Dhavale with over 2 decades of rich experience in manufacturing and trading over the years under his leadership, SAMARTH ENTERPRISES has grown & expanded its scale of operations exponentially and today it caters to Indian as well as multinational clients by best possible way.
